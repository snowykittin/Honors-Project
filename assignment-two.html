    Wireframing

    Deliverables

    • Wireframe

    Assignment Details

    This assignment included one main deliverable: a wireframe of the kiosk that students were asked to provide in the scenario for Assignment 1. The main purpose of a wireframe is to allow teams to better understand the visual product in a bare-bones format. Wireframes are typically extremely low-fidelity, meaning that they lack the visual flare that most finished products have. This is intentional, as their purpose is mainly for internal use. Wireframes can even be completed on paper, although online resources such as Whimsical have become popular for online wireframe creation. Everyone has a different mental image when being described something, and a wireframe helps everybody ensure they are on the same page, as well as allow for better visual organization of information. For this assignment, students had to prototype one process the kiosk may be used for, such as booking an event.

    Reflection

    If I had to name an assignment that I would’ve been able to do without taking this class, this would have been the first one to come to mind. Wireframing has been something I’ve been doing for several years, and I have the most familiarity with it. I also have familiarity with using Whimsical, the application of choice for creating this. However, my knowledge of wireframing was not as in-depth as I attempted to go for this particular assignment. Adding comments about the specific interactions that could be made was something I had always annotated elsewhere instead of on the actual points of interaction themselves, which was a change that I will be taking into account for any future wireframes that I create. I also fell into the unfortunate category of trying to make my wireframes way too complicated, which I scaled back for this assignment. Being able to take an existing skill and hone it into something much more efficient, but also much more applicable to its actual purpose is something I always appreciate taking the time to do. My high school computer science teacher wasn’t as interested in the design side of things as I was, which meant that a lot of that I was left on my own to discover. Naturally, being an amateur, there will be quite a few mistakes that I make. This wasn’t a perfect wireframe either, but a significant improvement over ones I had made in the past.

    This is a skill that is particularly useful for my field. While I may not be directly be the one creating the wireframes, having the capability to essentially scaffold out a website and make the connections as to what it is intended to do is beneficial. Even in the instance of trying to just pitch an idea to the rest of my team, not everybody visualizes things in the same way, and having a method of being able to jot down the picture in my brain provides a massive advantage. Another nice perk to wireframing is that you don’t have to have any massive talent in the artistic department to be able to use it to your advantage. Anybody can draw a box and some circles, and hopefully everybody can write English. Those fundamentals essentially are what make up wireframes.

    If I went back and redid this assignment, I would probably choose an alternative path of interaction. I felt that choosing an event as my task didn’t leave me with too many good options for wireframing. There were some interesting features that I had eluded to, such as sending maps via QR code, that I could have fleshed out their use. Even showing the highly interactive map would have been another great path to wireframe. I think that being able to consider alternate alternatives is always important when reflecting on a project, and in the case of this one, I feel I took the easy way out. I also would have liked to better detail the interactions, taking advantage of more of Whimsical’s features in the process. There is a lot of room for improvement in regards to my wireframing skills, but overall, I feel rather good about how I did on this particular assignment. It’s extremely relevant to what I want to do in the future. I think I also would try to use another product, as I did find myself running into the limitations of Whimsical. For its purpose, it accomplished it, but if I were to go above-and-beyond, I’d need to use an alternative.
